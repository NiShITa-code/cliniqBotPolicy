# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [18/12/2025]

This Privacy Policy explains how **[“CliniqBot”]** (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when users interact with our services via WhatsApp or related communication channels.

---

## 1. Introduction

DocBot is an AI-powered assistant that helps doctors and clinics manage patient communication, appointment scheduling, and clinical documentation through WhatsApp and related platforms.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal and medical data responsibly in compliance with applicable Indian laws and Meta (WhatsApp) Platform Policies.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

### a) Patient Information

- Name
- Phone number
- WhatsApp messages
- Voice notes or audio messages (if shared by the user)
- Appointment-related information
- Health or consultation-related details voluntarily shared by the user

### b) Doctor / Clinic Information

- Name
- Contact details
- WhatsApp business number
- Clinic details (if provided)

---

## 3. How We Use the Information

We use collected information only for legitimate operational purposes, including:

- Responding to patient queries
- Scheduling and managing appointments
- Transcribing voice messages into text
- Generating visit summaries or clinical notes for doctors
- Sending confirmations, reminders, and service-related messages
- Improving service quality and reliability

**We do NOT use personal or medical data for advertising or marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers strictly for operational purposes, such as:

- WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta)
- Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS or equivalent)
- AI services for speech-to-text or text processing

All third parties are required to handle data securely and only for the intended purpose.

**We do not sell, rent, or trade personal or health data.**

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secure servers with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.
- Access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel only.
- Reasonable security measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- Personal and medical data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service.
- Data may be deleted or anonymized upon request, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

---

## 7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

- Request access to their personal data
- Request correction or deletion of their data
- Withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the service

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

---

## 8. Legal Compliance (India)

We handle sensitive personal data in accordance with:

- The Information Technology Act, 2000
- Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011

Medical data is used strictly for facilitating healthcare communication and support.
