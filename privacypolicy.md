# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 18/12/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how **CliniqBot** ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when users interact with our services via WhatsApp or related communication channels.

---

## 1. Introduction

**CliniqBot** is an AI-powered assistant that helps doctors and clinics manage patient communication, appointment scheduling, and clinical documentation through WhatsApp and related platforms.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal and medical data responsibly in compliance with applicable Indian laws, including the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**, and Meta (WhatsApp) Platform Policies.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information:

### a) Patient Information

* Name
* Phone number
* WhatsApp messages
* Voice notes or audio messages (if shared by the user)
* Appointment-related information
* Health or consultation-related details voluntarily shared by the user

### b) Doctor / Clinic Information

* Name
* Contact details
* WhatsApp Business number
* Clinic details (if provided)

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing

We process personal data solely for lawful and legitimate purposes, including:

* Responding to patient queries
* Scheduling and managing appointments
* Transcribing voice messages into text
* Generating visit summaries or clinical notes for doctors
* Sending confirmations, reminders, and service-related messages
* Improving service quality, reliability, and system performance

**Personal and medical data is never used for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.**

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers strictly for operational purposes, including:

* Meta WhatsApp Business Platform
* Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS or equivalent)
* AI service providers for speech-to-text and text processing

All third parties are contractually obligated to:

* Process data only on our instructions
* Implement reasonable security safeguards
* Comply with applicable data protection laws

**We do not sell, rent, or trade personal or health data.**

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

* Data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit where applicable
* Access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel only
* Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse

---

## 6. Data Retention

* Personal and medical data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes
* Data may be deleted, anonymized, or archived upon user request unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business or compliance purposes

---

## 7. User Rights (DPDP Act, 2023)

In accordance with the DPDP Act, users have the right to:

* Access their personal data
* Request correction or erasure of their personal data
* Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the service
* Request grievance redressal

Requests can be made using the contact details provided below.

## 8. Consent

By interacting with CliniqBot via WhatsApp or related platforms, users explicitly consent to the collection and processing of their personal and medical data as described in this Privacy Policy.
